Here is a sample application for sick leave:

Dear [Manager's Name],

I am writing to request a sick leave of [number] days, starting from [date] to [date], due to health reasons.

Unfortunately, I am currently unwell and am unable to attend work. I am suffering from [briefly mention your illness or symptoms, e.g. fever, flu, stomach infection, etc.]. My doctor has advised me to take complete rest and undergo treatment, which will take [number] days.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will do my best to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better. I will keep you updated on my status and provide a medical certificate if required.

Thank you for your understanding and support during this time.

Sincerely,
Yash Sapkal
